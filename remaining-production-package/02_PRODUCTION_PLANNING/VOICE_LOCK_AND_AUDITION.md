# आवाज लक र Google Flow अडिसन

## प्रयोग विधि

Google Flow मा `Omni Flash` रोज्नुहोस्। प्रत्येक पात्रका लागि एउटा custom voice बनाउनुहोस्। तलको asset name ठ्याक्कै राख्नुहोस्, उपयुक्त preset base voice छान्नुहोस्, performance description paste गर्नुहोस्, sample line सुन्नुहोस्, अनि प्रयोगकर्ताले स्वीकृत गरेपछि मात्र voice lock मान्नुहोस्। त्यसपछि सम्बन्धित clip prompt मा `@Voice: NAME` प्रयोग गर्नुहोस्।

## मुख्य आवाजहरू

### `GG_KRISHNA_CHILD_01`

- पात्र: सात वर्षको बाल कृष्ण
- Base voice: उपलब्ध preset मध्ये स्पष्ट, कोमल, कम उमेर सुनिने child/young voice; adult male voice नछान्ने
- Performance description: `A warm, playful seven-year-old North Indian boy speaking natural, crystal-clear Hindi. Curious intelligence, gentle confidence, light smile in the voice, devotional warmth without theatrical exaggeration. Never adult-sounding, never squeaky, never cartoonish. Medium-soft volume, clean diction, natural pauses.`
- Sample: `बाबा... जो परंपरा सत्य पर टिकी हो, उसे निभाना चाहिए।`

### `GG_NANDA_FATHER_01`

- पात्र: नन्द बाबा
- Base voice: warm mature Indian male
- Performance description: `A warm middle-aged North Indian father speaking natural Hindi. Grounded village elder, calm authority, affectionate toward Krishna, thoughtful rather than pompous. Medium-low pitch, measured pace, clear consonants, emotionally honest.`
- Sample: `कन्हैया... तुम्हारी बात में तर्क है।`

### `GG_YASHODA_MOTHER_01`

- पात्र: यशोदा
- Base voice: warm mature Indian female
- Performance description: `A warm North Indian mother speaking natural Hindi. Loving, protective and emotionally expressive, with firm clarity when worried. Never melodramatic, never elderly, never overly polished like an advertisement.`
- Sample: `कन्हैया... सब सुरक्षित हैं ना?`

### `GG_INDRA_ROYAL_01`

- पात्र: इन्द्र
- Base voice: powerful mature Indian male
- Performance description: `A powerful royal Indian male speaking formal natural Hindi. Deep controlled resonance, proud authority and restrained danger. Anger builds from wounded pride rather than shouting; later remorse becomes quieter and human while preserving the same vocal identity. No monster growl, no trailer-announcer delivery.`
- Sample: `एक बालक... मेरे सम्मान को चुनौती देगा?`

### `GG_NARRATOR_01`

- पात्र: कथावाचक
- Base voice: assured adult Indian storyteller, gender to be chosen after audition
- Performance description: `An observant Indian mythological storyteller speaking clear literary Hindi. Intimate, assured and cinematic, never preachy, never like a television promo. Controlled pace, meaningful pauses, restrained wonder and tension.`
- Sample: `उसी समय व्रज में एक ऐसा निर्णय होने वाला था, जो स्वर्ग तक तूफान भेज देता।`

## सहायक आवाजहरू

### `GG_GOPA_ELDER_01`

`An elderly Braj village man speaking simple natural Hindi, weathered but clear, humble, sincere, never comic.`

Sample: `गोवर्धन का...`

### `GG_GOPA_ADULT_01`

`An adult Braj cowherd speaking natural Hindi, grounded and direct, medium pitch, emotionally responsive, never royal or polished.`

Sample: `यह साधारण बारिश नहीं है...`

### `GG_GOPI_ADULT_01`

`An adult Braj village woman speaking natural Hindi, caring and frightened under pressure, clear diction, dignified and never shrill.`

Sample: `कन्हैया... हमें बचा लो।`

### `GG_MESSENGER_01`

`A young adult male celestial messenger speaking respectful formal Hindi, breathless urgency without comedy, clear and deferential before Indra.`

Sample: `देवराज... व्रज में आपकी पूजा नहीं हुई।`
